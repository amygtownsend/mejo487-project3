Bicycle crash trends in Orange County, North Carolina

The League of American Bicyclists has ranked North Carolina as the 20th most bicycle-friendly state in the nation and Carrboro as the state's most bicycle-friendly community. However, even bikers in Carrboro and the surrounding county need to watch out for potential crashes with vehicles. Orange County had 194 reported bicycle crashes from 2007 to 2015.

Data from Orange County as a whole appears to show that bicyclists from ages 20 to 24 are involved the most frequently in bicycle crashes. When analyzing the crashes by their location in Chapel Hill, Carrboro or a rural area, it becomes evident that bicycle crashes in Chapel Hill are the cause of this trend. The high number of crashes with bikers in their early twenties is likely due to the large student population at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
